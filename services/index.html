Website Services

Interested in owning a website like this? Mackenzie is ready to work with you and can have your site up and running within a week.

The Process

Planning

We will work closely with you to develop a website that meets your needs and expectations. If you feel your needs can be discussed over email then this is okay. Otherwise, we can arrange a remote meeting to discuss your needs in more detail at no extra cost.

Development

During the process, each feature will be considered and implemented using the Agile methodology. This means that fully completed features will be delivered as a viewable prototype at an incredibly fast rate. This way you can closely watch the progress of your website and make sure it is developing to your liking.

Modifications

At the end of the development cycle, if there are any further modifications that you would like to see included, these will be discussed, developed and implemented as a final wrap-up.

Pricing

Variable pricing

The cost of a website is dependent on the features and complexity of the website, and as such will be discussed and confirmed in contact. However, expect to pay somewhere between £200 and £500 for a fully functional website. This includes all of the features discussed in the planning stage, as well as any modifications that you would like to see included.

You will be required to pay a deposit of 50% of the final price before work begins, and the remaining 50% upon completion. This is to ensure the security of both parties, and to cover development costs.

Discounts

If you can present a valid student ID, you will be entitled to a 20% discount on the final price of your website. This is to help students who are looking to start their own business or showcase their work.
